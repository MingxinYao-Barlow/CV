Mingxin Yao Ph.D.

My research lies at computaional linguistics, corpus linguistics, and discourse studies. Recently, I have focused on large-scale, diachronic corpus-based studies of academic and media discourse.

My work has appeared in journals such as Scientometrics, Written Communication, Digital Scholarship in the Humanities, Journal of Aging Studies, Ethics & Behavior, Corpora, and Foreign Language Teaching (China).
